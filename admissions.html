​Admission Process:   
  • Read all of the information on this site, including the Handbook.
  • Schedule an appointment to take a tour.  We do require a visit with your child before moving forward with the registration process.
  • Once you have completed the tour and  we've decided to move forward with our partnership, you will complete the registration packet and we will schedule the first day.  
     All required payments will be due upon submission of registration.


Ages:

We welcome children between the ages of 2.5-5
Children must be potty trained.

Dates and hours:

The Purposeful Preschool is open year round from 9-3:00

Closed for state and federal holidays (following the public school system calendar).
 Note: If the holiday falls on a Saturday we will be closed the Friday before.
If the holiday falls on a Sunday we will be closed the Monday after.

The school will be closed for 2 weeks out of every year for vacation.  One of those being a fixed winter break and the other changes yearly.
This week is planned before the next calendar year giving families ample time to plan alternative care.

Three Enrollment options are available:

Parents may choose the traditional full preschool day  from 9-12:30 and an extended day option until 3 pm.
